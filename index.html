Panther Chameleons

Panther Chameleons are cold blooded reptiles. Chameleons are a popular lizard but the Panther Chameleon is even more popular for the varius colors they grow up to have.

About Panther Chamleons

Quick Facts

  • Are arboreal lizards, meaning they prodominantly live in the trees
  • Have forked feet in order to be excellent climbers
  • found in the eastern and northern parts of Madagascar in a tropical forest
  • Use their unique color changing to better camoflauge

Food

  • Eat various types of bugs, making them insectivores
  • When they are very hungry they will occsionally eat plant vegetation
  • They are slow but efficient at hunting due to their fast, sticky and accurate tongues
